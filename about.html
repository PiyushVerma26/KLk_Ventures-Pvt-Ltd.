Meet the Visionary Behind Klk Ventures Pvt Ltd.

Akshat Jain

CEO

KLK Ventures, formerly known as "KL Khanna Gear Industries Pvt. Ltd.," is a key player in heavy industrial gears manufacturing. After a rebranding as KLK Ventures Pvt Ltd., led by an experienced CEO, the company expanded into Heavy Engineering and Energy Conversion. In 2018, KLK Ventures made a significant move by establishing a battery manufacturing plant and in-house testing facility. Thanks to the CEO's expertise in energy, import-export, accounting, trading, and strategic planning, KLK has become a preferred energy partner. The company is recognized for providing energy storage, efficient lighting, and innovative solar solutions to government, PSUs, and private organizations. KLK Ventures is actively involved in offering innovative energy solutions for rural development, showcasing its commitment to making a positive impact in diverse sectors.
