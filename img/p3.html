CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILTY

The group believes corporate social responsibility (CSR) is a critical mission that is at the heart of everything that it does, how it thinks and what it is. We are committed to integrating environmental, social and ethical principles into the core business, thereby enhancing long-term stakeholder value and touching the lives of over thousands of people.

The group is focussed on CSR programmes that aims to be relevant to local, national and global contexts, keep disadvantaged communities as the focus, be based on globally-agreed sustainable development principles and be implemented in partnership with governments, NGOs and other relevant stakeholders.

The CSR approach helps us envisages to the group and executes strategies to support communities in partnership with governments and civil society. Key to this approach is our employees who generously give of their time, experience and talent to serve communities; and NGO’s and companies encourage and facilitate us to do so. CSR is amongst the top 10 corporate volunteering programmes in the world.
